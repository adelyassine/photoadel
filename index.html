MY COURSES

At Photo Adel, we offer a range of educational courses in professional photography, video editing principles, master video editing with supplies, and cross-sectional (drone) photography. Committed to excellence, we strive to exceed your expectations, thus providing the best service.

Photography course

Learn the basics of photography, including the lighting triangle (ISO), shutter speed, and aperture. Learn the rules of the composition triangle. Creativity and thinking. Lighting. A detailed explanation of the world of cameras and lenses. Practical training and job market qualification.

videoography course
